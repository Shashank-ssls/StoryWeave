"The Hollow Crown" — sample novel for StoryWeave
================================================

This short, original work of fiction was written specifically as test/demo data
for StoryWeave. It is released into the public domain under the Creative Commons
CC0 1.0 Universal dedication: https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

To the extent possible under law, the author has waived all copyright and related
or neighboring rights to this work. You may copy, modify, distribute, and use it,
even for commercial purposes, all without asking permission.

It is deliberately alias-rich and contains identity/secret elements (a hidden
royal heir, a spymistress operating under an alias, and a transmigration hint) so
that later phases — coreference clustering, the reveal fence, and Tier-3 identity
inference — have something real to exercise. Real or copyrighted novels must NOT
be committed; they belong in the gitignored data/raw/ directory.
